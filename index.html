About me

I'm a third year master student in Peking University supervised by Prof. Jinan Sun. I received the B.S. degree from University of Science and Technology Beijng, in 2021. My main research interests include machine learning and computer vision, such as representation learning, challenging image retrieval, and multi-modal prompt learning. Recently, I focus on the area of AI for science, such as physical simulation. Feel free to contact me for communication and collaboration.
